Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Lasagna is a hearty, comforting Italian classic known for its rich layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel (or ricotta), and melted cheese. Each bite offers a perfect balance of textures and flavors—tender noodles, robust tomato-based meat sauce, and gooey cheese all baked to bubbly, golden perfection. This dish is a staple in many households and is perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers, where the flavors deepen even more.

Homemade lasagna may take a bit of time to prepare, but the end result is well worth the effort. Whether you go traditional with beef and pork, or prefer variations like vegetarian or chicken, the beauty of lasagna lies in its versatility. With each comforting layer, it delivers a taste of home, making it ideal for family dinners, holidays, or any time you want to serve something deeply satisfying and full of love.

Ingredients

  • For the Meat Sauce

    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 lb ground beef
    • 1 can crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • Salt and pepper

  • For the Cheese Layer

    • 1.5 cups ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and pepper

  • Other

    • 9-12 lasagna noodles
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Extra Parmesan for topping

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce
    • In a large skillet. heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add onion and cook until softened (about 5 minutes).
    • Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
    • Add in ground meat and cook until browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stiring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, parmesan, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Cook the Noodles
    • Boil according to package instructions, drain, and lay flat on parchment or oiled surface.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
    • Preheat oven to 375 F or 190 C.
    • In a 9 x 13 inch baking dish
      1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
      2. Add a layer of noodles.
      3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture.
      4. Add meat sauce.
      5. Sprinkle mozzarella.
      6. Repeat layers (usually 3 total), ending with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil (spray underside with cooking spray to prevent sticking).
    • Bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake an additional 15-20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
    • Let rest 10-15 minutes before slicing.
